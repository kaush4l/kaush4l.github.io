## Software Developer at Cerner Corporation

**Period**: August 2018 - Present

I joined Cerner Corporation as a Software Developer after completing intensive training on cutting-edge technologies. Cerner is a global leader in healthcare technology, and working here has significantly shaped my professional growth.

### Responsibilities & Achievements

- Developed and maintained enterprise healthcare applications serving thousands of users
- Collaborated with cross-functional teams including designers, QA engineers, and product managers
- Participated in agile development processes with sprint planning, code reviews, and continuous integration
- Contributed to large-scale codebases while maintaining code quality and following best practices

### Key Learnings

**Team Collaboration**: Working with a large, diverse team taught me the importance of clear communication, documentation, and coordinated effort. I learned to balance individual contributions with team goals.

**Professional Development**: The role provided exposure to enterprise-level software development, including:
- Scalable architecture patterns
- Healthcare domain knowledge
- Security and compliance requirements
- Performance optimization at scale

**Technology Stack**: Gained expertise in modern web technologies, backend systems, and cloud services while working on production applications used in healthcare settings.

### Growth & Impact

The position has been instrumental in developing my professional skills, from technical expertise to soft skills like stakeholder communication and project management. The fast-paced environment and complex problem domain continue to provide valuable learning opportunities.
